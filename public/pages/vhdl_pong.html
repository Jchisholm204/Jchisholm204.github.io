FPGA: Pong

Recreating the famous old "Pong" game in VHDL. Running on an Altera DE2

Premise

In order to further my knowledge of FPGA logic and VHDL I created a simple Pong game.
While simple on the surface, this project required three clocked processes, a VGA signal generator and some interesting logic.
